Welcome to SMU Centre for Research on International Trade

The SMU Centre for Research on International Trade focuses on research that maps the complex network of firm-to-firm connections to analyze how global disruptions affect Singapore’s trade ecosystem. By examining how external shocks spread through these links, we aim to better understand their impact on firm productivity, labor demand, and innovation. These insights are essential for developing strategies that help strengthen the resilience and competitiveness of Singapore’s economy in the global trade environment.
