2026 SMU Annual Trade Conference

Dear Colleagues:

The 2026 SMU Annual Trade Conference will take place on May 25–26, 2026, for two full days at Singapore Management University (SMU). The conference is organized by the SMU Centre for Research on International Trade, which promotes research analyzing the complex networks of firm-to-firm connections in Singapore and other Asian economies.

We invite submissions of high-quality papers in fields including, but not limited to, international trade, network economics, spatial econometrics, and labor economics. In particular, we welcome papers that examine the role of firm-to-firm input-output linkages in transmitting aggregate shocks and their implications for the firm-level productivity, labor demand, and innovation activities.

The conference program will include parallel academic sessions organized around the Centre’s core research themes - firm-level production networks, global value chains, labor markets, and innovation - as well as a policy panel discussion that will be open to participants from the public sector and industry.
